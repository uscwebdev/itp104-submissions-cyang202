F/W 23 Fashion Trends

Fasion trends in Fall/Winter 2023 have undergone a large shift from previous years. The colorful, sporty, and kitsch styles of Y2K fashion, which was one of the major trends in 2022, have been replaced by simple and basic designs. Numerous fashion brands have placed focus on timeless styles this season, using less decorations on their clothes to create a simple look. Most trending outfits this season also use monochrome or neutral colors as their main colors, which adds on to the creation of a simple, timeless design.
Keywords of This Season
Classic
Classic looks are timeless: though their outfits include simple designs that might seem plain, they do not look outdated and look stylish in any period of time. Classic outfits also convey an elegant, high-class mood that is associated with the old money aesthetic. Most classic designs use monochrome colors such as black, gray, and white, and neutral colors, such as ivory, beige, navy, and brown. Examples of classic outfits include basic cardigans, sweaters, straight plants, pleated skirts, flats, and loafters.
Black and White
Inspired by the popularity of simple outfits, as well as the office core style, black and white clothing have also been a major fashion trend this season. The combination of black and white - two monochrome colors that are highly contrasting - creates a modern look. Black and white also suits the mood of winter, which is mostly associated with coldness and lack of color.
Silhouette
Numerous outfits that reveal body silhouettes have been presented on runway shows of many fashion brands this season. Most of these clothings incorporate a wider design on the top and bottom and a narrower design on the waist, resembling an hourglass shape. Due to the popular trend in outfits that emphasize body silhouettes, skirts - which are more voluminous than pants - have become a major fashion item this season. Accessories such as belts, which divide the upper and lower parts of the body and empahsize the waist are also popular.
