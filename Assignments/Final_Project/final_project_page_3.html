Colors/Material

Colors
Most popular colors this season are neutral tone, such as gray, camel, brown, and ivory. These colors go well with a wide range of other colors, making neutral tone clothes suitable to match with any type of clothing. Monochrome colors such as gray and black can be paired with most other colors as well. The only vibrant color that is trending this season is red: representing passion and excitement, red can be worn to add a vibrant mood to the neutral and monochrome outfits.

Material
Some of the trending materials used for clothing this season include leather, wool, mesh, and padded clothes. Leather, wool, and padded clothes are fabric/material that adds warmth to our clothes, which are suitable for the fall/winter season. Although mesh does not give as much warmth, they add texture to the classic outfits that are popular this season and create a design that is not too simple or plain.
